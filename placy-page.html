FAQ

How long does it take to get approved by WhatsApp/Telegram/Other messenger or tool to use Placy Pro?

Placy Pro manages the setup and compliance process for you and in case of WhatsApp the approval usually takes a few days to a week. For other channels and tools you can expect either instant approval or up to one day. Learn more about the specific approval timelines from your manager.

How do we measure ROI between the two approaches?

Measure time-to-value (how quickly leads are handled), lead conversion rate (quality after automation), agent time saved, and maintenance costs. Placy Pro typically shows quicker uplift in response times and conversion due to prebuilt qualification and phone/voice support; internal builds often show slower initial ROI and higher ongoing engineering expense.

I can create a chatbot myself using ChatGPT. Why do I need Placy?

While tools like ChatGPT can be used to build basic chatbots, creating a fully functional, scalable, and integrated solution requires time, expertise, and ongoing maintenance. Placy saves you time and effort by providing a turnkey solution that’s ready to deploy, with advanced features and continuous updates to stay ahead of your business needs.

‍

Can Placy Pro personalize messages for different leads?

Absolutely. Messages can be dynamically personalized based on user name, inquiry type, language, and other lead attributes available to Placy Pro.

How much customization or engineering is required to get started?

Minimal for standard real-estate use cases. Because Placy ships with industry workflows and modules, onboarding is faster and requires less custom dev compared with a generic setup. Custom integrations or advanced workflows are available if you need them.
